Yellow Jacket Jeopardy!

Word Bank Theme:

"SPORTS & GAMES"

CLICK TO GUESS!

Instructions:


1. Press a letter on the keyboard to guess that letter

2. If your letter is in the word, it will show up in the correct spot

3. Guess wrong and your letter will show up underneath the empty spaces!

4. Once you have as many incorrect guesses as the number of letters in the secret word, you lose! (i.e. if the word has 5 letters, you lose after 5 incorrect guesses)

5. Make a guess at the secret word any time by clicking the box in the top right of the game board.
